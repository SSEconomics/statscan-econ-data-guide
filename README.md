# statscan-econ-data-guide
## Statistics Canada data links for economics

Finding the right series on the Statistics Canada website can be surprisingly time-consuming. This section collects the main tables I use to build macro and applied economics datasets for my courses. All links point to the official Statistics Canada (or Bank of Canada) sources.

---

### National Accounts

- **Real (chained dollars) and nominal (current) quarterly GDP and components (1961Q1–present)**  
  Statistics Canada, Table 36-10-0104-01  
  <https://doi.org/10.25318/3610010401-eng>

  **Textbook components (C, I, G, X, M, Y):**

  - `C` = Household final consumption expenditure  
    \+ Non-profit institutions serving households (NPISH) final consumption expenditure  
  - `I` = Business gross fixed capital formation  
    \+ NPISH gross fixed capital formation  
    \+ Investment in inventories  
  - `G` = General governments final consumption expenditure  
    \+ General governments gross fixed capital formation  
  - `X` = Exports of goods and services  
  - `M` = Imports of goods and services  
  - `Y` = Gross domestic product (GDP)
